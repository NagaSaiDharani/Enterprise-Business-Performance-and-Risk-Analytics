## Business Objectives

The primary objectives of this project are:

1. Track enterprise revenue performance against predefined targets
2. Identify sales trends across regions, products, and customer segments
3. Monitor operational efficiency through delay analysis
4. Quantify business risk using complaint severity and refund indicators
5. Enable leadership to quickly drill into problem areas using interactive dashboards

The solution is designed for executive leadership, operations managers, 
and risk teams to support data-driven decision-making.
