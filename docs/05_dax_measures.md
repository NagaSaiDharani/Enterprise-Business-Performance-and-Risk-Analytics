## Key DAX Measures

Revenue & Sales:
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Revenue Growth %
- Revenue Achievement %
- Revenue Status

Operations:
- Delayed Orders %
- Average Delay Days
- On-Time Delivery %

Risk & Complaints:
- Total Complaints
- High Risk Complaints %
- Refund Rate
- Risk Status

All measures were written using DAX and optimized for filter context 
across regions, products, and time.
