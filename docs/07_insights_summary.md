## Key Business Insights

- Revenue performance shows variation across regions, with specific regions consistently underperforming targets.
- Operational delays have a measurable downstream impact on customer complaints and refunds.
- High-severity complaints, while fewer in volume, contribute disproportionately to financial risk.
- Certain product categories exhibit higher delay and complaint rates, indicating process improvement opportunities.
- Proactive monitoring of operational KPIs can significantly reduce risk exposure.

These insights demonstrate how integrated analytics can move organizations 
from reactive reporting to proactive performance management.
